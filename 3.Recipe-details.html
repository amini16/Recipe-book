Chocolate cake

ingredients:

  • flour:1/3cups
  • sugar:1/2 cups
  • cocoa powder: 3/4 cup
  • Baking powder: 1/2 teaspoons
  • Baking soda: 1/2
  • salt:1/0 teaspoon
  • Eggs:2
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Vegetable oil: 1/2
  • Vanilla extract:1 teaspoons
  • Hot water: 1 cup

instructions:

  1. preheat the oven to 350F (175C)
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, suger, Cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the hot water and beat until the batter is smooth. ti well be a little thin- that's normal.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Check with a toothpick- if it comes out clean, the cake is done.
  7. Let the cake cool before slining or decorating with melted chocolate.

Orange juice

ingredients

  • Fresh orange: 3 to 4 (for one glass of juice)
  • Suger (optional): 1-2 tablespoons
  • Ice cubes: a few (optional)
  • Water: half a glass(optional, to dilute)

instructions

  1. Wash the Orange:

    2. Rinse the oranges well to remove any dirt.

  2. Juice the oranges:

    3. Cut the oranges in half and squeeze them using a manual or electric juicer. if you don't have a iuicer, you can squeeze by hand and strain the juice to remove the pulp.

  3. Add Sugar (optional):

    4. If the oranges are too sour, you can add 1-2 tablespoons of suger and stir and until it dissolves.

  4. Add ice or water(optional)

    5. Add a few ice cubes if you want it chilled. You can also add a bit of water to make it lighter.

  5. Serve:

    6. Pour the juice into a glass and serve immediately to retain the Vitamin C.

Burger

ingredients

  • Ground beef: 250 grame
  • Grated onion: 1 medium
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
  • Garlic powder or minced garlic (optionl): a little
  • Oil or butter: for frying
  • Burger buns: 2
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese slices, mayonnaise and ketchup: for serving

instructions

  1. Prepare the burger mixture

    2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with grated onion, salt, pepper, and garlic powder if using. knead well until the ingredients are well combined.

  2. Shape the patties:

    3. Divide the mixture into tow equal parts and shape them into flat, round burger patties.

  3. Fry the burgers:

    4. Heat a little oil or butter in a frying pan. Cook the patties on both sides until they are well cooked and browned.

  4. Warm the buns:

    5. Slightly warm the burger buns and add lettuce, tomato, pickles, Cheese, and your favorite sauces like mayo and ketchup.

    Serve and Enjoy

Beef steak

ingredients

  • Beef fillet or sirion steak: 1 piece (about 200-250g)
  • Salt and black pepper: to teate
  • Olive oil or butter: 1-2 tablespoon
  • Crushed garlic (optional): 1 clove
  • Fresh or dried rosemary(optional): a little
  • Grilled Vegetable for serving: such as tomatoes, lettuce or bell peppers
  1. Prepare the steak:

    2. Pat the steak dry with a paper towel. Season both sides with salt and black pepper. Optionally, rub with garlice and rosemary for extra flavor.

  2. Heat the gill pan:

    3. Preheat a grill pan or cast iron skillet until very hot. Add a bit of olive oil or butter.

  3. Cook the steak:

    4. place the steak in the hot pan. Cook each side for about 2 to 4 minutes, depending on your preferred doneness (are, medium, or well-done).

  4. Let it rest:

    5. After cooking, remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 3 to 5 minutes. This helps keep it juiciy and tender.

  5. Serve:

    6. Serve the steak with grilled vegetables or salad and some fries if you like. you can also top it with a small piece of butter for extra flavor.
